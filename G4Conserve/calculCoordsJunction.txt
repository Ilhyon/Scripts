For the start the position < junction length so :

Formula :
position = startIntron - 1 - EXTENSION + position

For the en the position > junction length so :
Formula :
position = endIntron + 1 - EXTENSION + position - 1
end = 17 + 1 - 5 + 10 - 1 = 22
